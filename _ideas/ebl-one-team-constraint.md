# Why Playing with Less Makes It More Fun

## The Idea
Most fantasy baseball leagues let you pick players from all 30 MLB teams. EBL (Experimental Baseball League) limits everyone to one team — the Philadelphia Phillies. That single constraint changes everything about how the game is played and why it's fun.

## Why It's Interesting
Restrictions sound like they'd make a game worse, but the opposite is true here. When everyone is fighting over the same 32 players, you have to know your team deeply. Every roster move matters. A bench player who gets called up is suddenly a hot commodity. This is a great example of how constraints in design — games, software, or life — often produce more interesting outcomes than total freedom.
