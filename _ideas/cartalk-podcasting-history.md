# Car Talk Invented the Podcast Before Podcasting Existed

## The Idea
Car Talk was a call-in radio show. But its format — two opinionated hosts, a specific topic, listener questions, recorded episodes you could catch later — is essentially what we now call a podcast. NPR's decision to archive and distribute Car Talk episodes online made it one of the first shows people downloaded and listened to on their own schedule. It's a story about how a new medium was invented without anyone noticing.

## Why It's Interesting
Podcasting wasn't invented by a tech company. It emerged from the collision of radio, the internet, and the MP3 format. Car Talk was perfectly positioned to make that jump because it was already episodic, already conversational, and already had a devoted following that wanted more control over when they listened. Understanding that history makes the current podcast explosion make more sense.

## Key Points to Cover
- What Car Talk was as a radio show: live call-in, weekly, NPR
